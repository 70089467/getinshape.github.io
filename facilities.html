Swimming pool

A fantastic cardiovascular sport, swimming works not only your heart to improve stamina, but many other muscles in the body such as the arms, legs and core. Suitable for all ages and fitness abilities, swimming is a low-impact way to burn calories and tone up. At GetInShape, our pools are open daily and offer lane swimming without the need to book, and some of our in-gym swimming pools even offer triathlon training classes.
